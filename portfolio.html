WELCOME TO SAMITOLAF FASHION PLACE

Samitolaf fashion place is an eco-friendly company providing training in fashion and beauty services for vulnerable people. Established in 2017, we design and produce our own products in our office and factory both located in Ikorodu Lagos state. Our entire production is proudly made in Nigeria.
